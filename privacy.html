Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information collected by 17 Ways Pty Ltd ACN 603890179 ("we”, “us” and “our”) via the website located at 17ways.com.au (“the Website”). The terms “you” and “your” refer to (i) you as a Website user personally, and (ii) if you are accessing the Website within the scope of your employment and/or on behalf of a company, that employer and/or company as appropriate.

Your privacy is important to us. By using the Website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Please read the following details carefully.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the treatment of your personal information, you can contact us at info@17ways.com.au.

Collection of Information

We may collect personal information from you, such as your name, address, phone number, email address, occupation, company, title, position and interests when you use the Website or do business with us (such as making a request for our services). We may collect cookies from your computer, which enables us to tell how and when you use the Website. This information can include your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times, pages within the Website which you visit and referring Website addresses. You have the ability to accept or decline cookies on your computer. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies. If you choose to decline cookies, your experience of the Website and, in particular, any interactive features of the Website, may be restricted and/or not possible. Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by us. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. Purposes of collection: The purposes for which we collect personal information about you include (i) to improve our services offered to you and to provide you with information about the services we offer; (ii) to provide the services outlined in the Terms of Use and deliver any services you have requested; (iii) to maintain, facilitate adapt and enhance the functioning of the Website and/or our services; and (iv) to provide general statistics regarding use of the website.

All provision of personal information is optional. However, if you do not provide us with certain personal information and/or you decline cookies on your computer, you may be unable to enjoy the full functionality including (but not limited to) being unable to access or use certain functionality and certain pages of the Website. Non-identifiable information may be collected regarding you in order to monitor website usage, visitor numbers, pages viewed, frequency and time spent on different website pages. Non-identifiable information may be used for business development and analysis purposes.

Non-identifiable information is not protected by this Privacy Policy and may be shared with advertisers and other third parties.

Disclosure

We do not sell, rent or lease our customer lists to third parties. We may share data with trusted partners for the limited purposes of helping us perform statistical analysis, helping us operate the Website (including without limitation website hosting), providing us with technology solutions, sending you email or postal mail or providing customer support.

Except as set out above, where the trusted partners are provided with your personal information for a limited purpose, we will only disclose personal information to an unrelated third party:
