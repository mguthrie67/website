Our Purpose

17 Ways was created to provide a range of technology services to the Australian and Asian market. As a small independent company with a wealth of experience across a range of technologies and business areas we can provide advice and services to your business, whatever problems you may face. Our overheads are a lot lower than many of our competitors which allows us to not only provide a really great service, but to do it at a competitive price.

Our Background

The senior partners of 17 Ways - Mark, Tim and John, all began working for Macquarie Bank around 1997. Over the next 17 years they worked on a wide range of activities including infrastructure design and operations, architecture, company mergers, joint ventures, multi-year development programs, technology and organisational change and offshoring. Prior to Macquarie they worked in industries as diverse as health care, insurance and government.
Our experience, skills and contacts serve us well in serving you.

Our Name

The number 17 comes from the number of years that we all spent at Macquarie. The compass logo and the name 17 Ways reflects our experience and our ability to look at all of the options such as the 16 directions on a standard compass, and to come up with one more. Our slogan is Options. Solutions. Results. which represents our approach to solving problems. We gather information and come up with the best options, then we deliver the solution leading to the best results for our customers. Or some shit like that anyway.
