What We Do

17 Ways are a technology firm based in Sydney. We provide a range of services to medium and large scale Australian businesses. We focus on the following three services:

Hardware 17 Ways are a reseller of converged, hyper-converged and commodity hardware solutions including VCE, SimpliVity and Avaya technology. In addition to providing the best solutions for your business we can also provide integration and migration support and can advise on the changes required to get the most out of your infrastructure solutions. Software 17 Ways offer a full range of software development and maintenance services as well as being a reseller for software such as ScienceLogic's EM7 monitoring system. With senior staff based in Sydney and access to a team of highly skilled programmers and testers, we can provide a high quality service to companies that need custom software development. Advice Technology is increasingly complex and business critical. Making the wrong technology decisions has far reaching consequences for any business. At 17 Ways we have the experience to help you across a range of technical areas including infrastructure, applications, integration, operations, organisational structure and strategy. We can provide advice or implement part or all of the solution for you.

About Us

We first met while working together at Macquarie Bank in 1997. During the course of the next 17 years we each had very successful careers delivering options, solutions and results across all of the businesses within the Macquarie Group. We left Macquarie at the end of 2014 and formed 17 Ways with a mission to use our skills, experience and contacts to provide technology solutions to Australian businesses.
